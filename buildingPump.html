Building the Pump

For this section you will need:

Parts

  • 5 M4x10 screws
  • 1 widget

Tools

  • 1 No. 2 Phillips screwdriver

Step 1: Put screws into the widget

  • Get a No. 2 Phillips screwdriver ready
  • Take three M4x10 screws and screw them into the widget

Step 2: Put more screws into the widget

  • Find the screwdriver again
  • Take two more M4x10 screws and screw them into the same widget
